About

I study the interstellar medium (ISM) in the Milky Way, focusing on the distribution of dust and gas, turbulence, extinction, and the structure of molecular clouds. I use Gaia-based 3D dust-extinction maps to derive density PDFs and study how cloud structure connects to star formation. Beyond research, I’m passionate about outreach and public science. Creativity is important to me—I work in photography, videography, music, and graphic design, often blending them with astronomy’s visual and emotional depth. I’m also drawn to philosophy, evolution, and history—the quiet patterns that connect how we understand the universe.

Highlights

  • Research interests: Multi-wavelength ISM, turbulence, star formation, 3D dust, statistical cloud structure.
  • Education: PhD (Astrophysics) — Chalmers; MSc (Gravity & Cosmology) — SBU; BSc (Astrophysics) — IAU.
