About

I study the interstellar medium (ISM) in the Milky Way, focusing on the distribution of dust and gas, turbulence, extinction, and the structure of molecular clouds. I use and analyze Gaia-based 3D dust-extinction maps to derive density PDFs and compare 3D vs 2D statistics to study nearby molecular clouds.

Highlights

  • Research interests: Multi-wavelength ISM, 3D dust/gas, turbulence, star formation, data analysis & ML in astronomy and outreach.
  • Education: PhD (Astrophysics) - Chalmers (2024– ); MSc (Gravity & Cosmology) - Shahid Beheshti University of Tehran (SBU); BSc (Astrophysics) - Azad University of Varamin-Pishva (IAU).
